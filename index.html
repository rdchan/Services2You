F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

Click on "request help" or "make a request" and fill out the form. After you have filled out the form the page will reload, indicating your submission has been processed. The respective service providers will reach out based on your requirements.

Click on "I can help" in the services tab to add your service to our database. Completing the form will allow you to have access to clients requests as they are submitted. If you are able to help out the customer, reach out to them using the contanct information they provided.

If you are a customer, completed the form, and have not heard back, click on "view map" to find servicers in your area to help you out. Their service and contact info have been provided for you to reach out to them.

Both servicers and plumbers can fill out the form multiple times. We request that you only fill out multiple forms if you are requesting a different service or fulfilling multiple fields.

After a customer in need fills out the form, servicers will recieve the information given that their service abiltiies match the necesarry requirements. For example, if a customer needs plumbing service, the servicers who provide the plumbing service will recieve the information. Servicers are then able to reach out to customers through the contact info. If you are a customer and do not hear from a servicer, click on "view map" to find servicers in your area to help you out. Their service and contact info have been provided for you to reach out to them.
